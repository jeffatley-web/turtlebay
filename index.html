Overview

AquaRev Water technology augmented new, state-of-the-art water treatment plants for optimal performance. Compliance, cost and ESG goals demanded more.

In 2021, AquaRev Water engineers were introduced to the Director of Engineering at Turtle Bay Resort — The Ritz-Carlton, O'ahu — to present the AquaRev Water device to the property's engineering and management teams. Despite recently completing the installation of new, state-of-the-art water treatment plants on property, the resort had three priorities the existing infrastructure couldn't fully address:

  • Sustainable, ESG-aligned water and energy management
  • Effective, lasting cost savings on pool operations
  • Heightened compliance with local government water-treatment standards for commercial pool operations

After a full technology review covering AquaRev's patents, laboratory results, and prior performance reports, a pilot program was approved.

The Pilot

32,000-gallon pool pilot — and implementation to all 5 pools and 2 spas across the property based on performance.

The pilot was designed to prove out impact under the resort's real operating conditions — not lab conditions — with all measurement performed by Turtle Bay's own engineering and accounting teams.

Pilot Asset
One commercial pool, 32,000 gallons — selected as a representative high-volume property pool.
Device
2" AquaRev Water unit installed on circulation line in 2021.
Pre-Install Baseline
Water-treatment logs, water-condition readings, and energy-use data captured in-house by Engineering. Three prior years of expense invoices pulled by in-house accounting for chemical-cost comparison.
Operational Tuning
AquaRev engineers recommended pump-flow reduction while maintaining U.S. Department of Health cycle requirements — installed alongside a data-logging device on the pump system to track pre- and post- behavior.
Post-Install Review
In-house data logs collected and compared against baseline + 3-year invoice history to produce a clean before/after picture.
Outcome
Pilot success → Resort installed AquaRev devices across all pools and spas on property.

Methodology & sourcing

How the numbers were measured.

  • Pilot: 2" AquaRev Water unit installed on one 32,000-gallon resort pool circulation line, mid-June 2022. Data-logger on the pump system; pump-flow tuned to maintain U.S. Department of Health turnover requirements.
  • Baselines: water-treatment logs, condition readings, and three years of chemical purchase invoices supplied by Turtle Bay Engineering and Accounting.
  • Cost savings net of inflation: chemical unit prices rose 2021→2022 across all categories. Reductions reflect volume use, not price.
  • Utility rates: water $14.90/1,000 gal (BWS commercial + Honolulu sewer, 2026); energy $0.34/kWh (HECO Schedule J avg, 2025).
  • Validation: all six published reductions appear in Turtle Bay Resort's 2022 ESG & Governance Report (pg. 10).
